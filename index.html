American Football

Goal

The goal of American football is to do the following:

  • Move the ball towards the opposition's end zone and ultimately into their end zone which results in a touchdown.
  • Advancing the ball into the end zone by running or throwing the ball downfield results in a touchdown.
  • Kicking the ball through the uprights set up in the end zone results in a field goal.
  • The team that scored more points at the end of four quarters wins the game.
